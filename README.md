# 📚 Askuala Lens (አስኳላ ሌንስ)

## 🚀 Overview
Askuala Lens is an AI-powered educational system designed to improve teaching and learning in Ethiopian classrooms. It helps teachers reduce manual work and enables students to better understand lessons through simple and smart digital tools.

---

## ❗️ Problem
In many schools, teaching still depends on manual processes such as handwritten assignments and paper-based records. This makes grading slow, delays feedback, and makes it difficult for students to understand lessons and track their progress.

---

## 💡 Solution
Askuala Lens introduces a digital platform that uses AI to automate grading, provide instant feedback, and explain learning content in local languages. It creates a more efficient and student-friendly learning environment.

---

## ⚙️ How the System Works (Workflow)

### 👨‍🏫 For Teachers:
1. Teacher logs into the system  
2. Uploads or scans student assignments  
3. System processes the content using AI  
4. Suggested grades are generated  
5. Teacher reviews and confirms grades  
6. Results are saved and shared with students  

### 👩‍🎓 For Students:
1. Student logs into the system  
2. Scans notes or textbooks  
3. Receives simple explanations in local language  
4. Views homework, tasks, and deadlines  
5. Takes quizzes and gets instant feedback  
6. Tracks personal performance and progress  

---

## ✨ Key Features
- 📸 Scan and grade handwritten assignments  
- 🌍 Multilingual explanations (Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, Tigrinya)  
- 📊 Attendance and performance tracking  
- 📝 Homework and daily task planner  
- 🧠 Online quizzes and instant results  
- 💬 Feedback system between students and teachers  
- 🔔 Notifications for updates and deadlines  

---

## 🎯 Goal
To make education more efficient, accessible, and interactive by reducing teachers’ workload and helping students learn faster and more effectively.

---

## 💡 Conclusion
Askuala Lens provides a simple but powerful solution to modern classroom challenges. By combining AI with practical features, it supports a more organized and effective learning experience for both students and teachers.
